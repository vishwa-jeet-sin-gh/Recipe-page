Simple omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic
eggs cooked to perfection. optionally filled with your cho
or meats.

Prepartion time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cook

Instuctions

  1. eat the eggs:Gather all the ingredients you will need, including 2-3 eggs,
    salt and pepper to taste, 1-2 tablespoons of milk if you like, butter or oil for cooking,
    and your choice of fillings such as cheese, vegetables,
    or ham.
  2. Heat the pan:Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
    Add a small amount of butter or oil, and let it melt,
    making sure it coats the pan evenly.
  3. Cook the omelettePour the beaten eggs into the heated skillet.
    Allow the eggs to cook undisturbed for a few seconds
    until they begin to set around the edges.
  4. Add fillings (optional):When the eggs start to set but are still slightly runny on top,
    evenly distribute your desired fillings over one half of the omelette.
    Continue cooking for another minute
    or until the eggs are mostly set but still slightly
    soft in the center.
  5. Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and proper if needed.
